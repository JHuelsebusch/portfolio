About Me

Hi, I am a frontend developer from Edewecht, Lower Saxony, Germany. To improve my web development skills, I attended an extensive programming school, where I also learned professional ways of working in programming. Meanwhile, I developed various web apps, from responsive websites to business apps and games.
You are welcome to visit and test my projects.

Digitization is becoming more and more important, including in my workplaces. At my previous jobs, among other, I was also responsible for optimizing processes or automation - but not for their direct implementation. I decided to take this step and to start my coding journey.

Before becoming a software developer, I completed an M. Sc. in chemistry and worked as a member of the quality management team or as a team leader in the lab. Now coding has become my passion and I look forward to learning more and more and getting new challenges.
